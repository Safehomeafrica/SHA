Our Thematic Areas

Evidence-based interventions structured around six critical areas of need in refugee communities

Protection

Mental Health & Psychosocial Support

Comprehensive counseling and trauma healing services for refugees affected by conflict and displacement.

Key Components:

  • Individual and group counseling sessions
  • Trauma healing workshops
  • Peer support networks
  • Referral system for specialized care
30+ Trained Counselors 1,200+ Sessions

Community-Based Child Protection

Safeguarding vulnerable children through awareness campaigns, safe spaces, and reporting mechanisms.

Key Components:

  • Child-friendly spaces
  • Community child protection committees
  • Prevention of GBV and exploitation
  • Case management system
500+ Children Reached 10 Safe Spaces

Peace Building

Community Peace Building

Promoting social cohesion and conflict resolution within the refugee and host communities.

Key Components:

  • Inter-community dialogue sessions
  • Conflict resolution training
  • Youth peace clubs
  • Cultural exchange programs
20 Peace Committees 500+ Participants

Economic Empowerment

Vocational training and income-generating activities for sustainable self-reliance.

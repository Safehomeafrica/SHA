About the Education Program

Safe Home Africa‚Äôs Education Program is a transformative initiative designed to break barriers and create opportunities for vulnerable youth in Nyumanzi Settlement. Our focus is on adolescent girls and teenage mothers, who often face unique challenges in accessing education. Through a blend of formal schooling, life skills training, and holistic support, we empower young women to reclaim their futures and become leaders in their communities.

  • Holistic Support: We provide counseling, mentorship, and safe spaces to nurture emotional well-being and personal growth.
  • Community Engagement: Our outreach programs sensitize families and local leaders about the importance of girls‚Äô education.
  • Skills for Life: From financial literacy to digital training, we equip youth with practical skills for success beyond the classroom.
  • Partnerships: Collaborations with clinics, schools, and local organizations amplify our impact and reach.

Who We Are

SAFE HOME AFRICA is a refugee youth-led community-based organization in Nyumanzi Settlement, Uganda. We empower young people to become leaders, advocates, and agents of change through health, education, and protection programs.

Our mission is to mobilize, support, empower, nurture and transform youth to become pillars of providing health care & psychosocial services with excellence through evidence-based practice in safe homes.

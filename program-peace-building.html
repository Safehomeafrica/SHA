About the Peace Building Program

Safe Home Africa's Peace Building Program is dedicated to fostering peaceful coexistence, social cohesion, and conflict resolution among refugees and host communities. Through innovative youth clubs, inter-community dialogues, and cultural exchange, we empower young leaders to become peace ambassadors. Our program addresses the root causes of conflict, builds trust, and creates safe spaces for learning, healing, and collaboration.

Key approaches: participatory workshops, peer mediation, leadership training, and community events.

Join us in building a future where every young person is a champion of peace!

Program Details

The Peace Building Program aims to foster understanding and cooperation among diverse communities, addressing the root causes of conflict and promoting peaceful coexistence. Our approach combines education, dialogue, and youth empowerment for lasting impact.
